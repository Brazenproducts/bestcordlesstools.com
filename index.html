The Ecosystem Lock-In Problem

Here's the thing nobody tells you at the hardware store: once you buy your first cordless tool, you're married to that battery platform. A $79 drill is really a $2,000+ decision when you factor in the 10-15 tools you'll eventually own.

That's why we built this site. Choosing between Milwaukee, DeWalt, Makita, and Ryobi isn't about one tool — it's about which ecosystem fits your work, your budget, and your future needs. We analyze the full picture so you don't end up with buyer's remorse three tools deep.

Our rankings prioritize tools with 4.5+ star Amazon ratings from 1,000+ verified reviews, low return rates, and the best price-to-quality ratio. We don't accept manufacturer sponsorships, and we never will.

For professionals: Milwaukee M18 FUEL is the benchmark. DeWalt 20V MAX XR is the close second with better pricing. For homeowners: Ryobi ONE+ is unbeatable on value.
